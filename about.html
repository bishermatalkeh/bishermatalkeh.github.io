Why AI in AEC?

Having spent years on construction sites and in university classrooms, I've seen firsthand how manual, time-consuming, and error-prone many AEC processes remain. From reviewing RFP documents to monitoring construction quality, there are countless opportunities where AI can improve efficiency, reduce errors, and enable better decision-making. This course allows me to explore these possibilities with a research-driven mindset and practical engineering perspective.
